POUR METHOD & BLOOM
FIRST 40% — SWEETNESS & ACIDITY
LAST 60% — STRENGTH
Tetsu Kasuya's 2016 World Brewers Cup method. First 40% sets flavor balance; last 60% sets strength. Coarser grind, slightly lower temp.
Chemex uses a thicker bonded filter — slower drawdown, coarser grind. Bloom + 2 pours. Total brew time ~5–6 minutes.
Immersion brew — all water added at once, coffee steeps, then press. No bloom. Pour immediately after pressing; don't let it sit or it keeps extracting.
