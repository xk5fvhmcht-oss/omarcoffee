RATIO & WATER MATH
ratio (coffee : water) 1 : 15
Why brew water ≠ cup yield: V60 grounds absorb roughly 2× their weight in water. With 20g coffee, ~40g stays in the puck. So you brew 340g to get 300g in the cup.

Tetsu Kasuya's 2016 World Brewers Cup method. First 40% of water sets flavor balance; last 60% sets strength. Coarser grind, slightly lower temp.

🌸 Bloom
PRE-INFUSION — DEGASSES THE COFFEE
bloom duration 30 s
Bloom water = 2× coffee weight (40g). Pour slowly in concentric circles, saturating all grounds. Fresh beans will bloom dramatically — that's CO₂ releasing. Older beans bloom less; that's fine.
